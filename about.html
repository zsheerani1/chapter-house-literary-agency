About the agency

Chapter House Literary Agency represents original fiction and works closely with writers to develop distinctive manuscripts for today’s publishing market.

Our agency was founded with a simple purpose: to champion stories with strong voices, emotional depth, and lasting impact. We believe great books begin with clear vision, thoughtful editorial support, and a strong partnership between author and agent.

What we look for

Distinctive Voice

We want writing that feels confident, engaging, and recognisable from the first page.

Strong Concept

We are drawn to stories with a clear premise, compelling stakes, and a strong sense of direction.

Emotional Depth

We look for fiction that offers more than plot alone, with characters and themes that stay with the reader.

Market Awareness

We value manuscripts that are original while still understanding where they fit within the contemporary publishing landscape.

Genres of interest

  • Literary fiction
  • Commercial and upmarket fiction
  • Crime and psychological suspense
  • Book club fiction
  • Speculative and genre-blending fiction
  • Selected historical fiction

Not currently seeking

At this time, we are not seeking children's picture books, poetry, screenplays, or academic non-fiction.
