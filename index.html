What we represent

Literary Fiction

We are drawn to thoughtful, voice-driven fiction with emotional depth and strong character development.

Crime and Suspense

We welcome tightly plotted mysteries, psychological suspense, and commercial thrillers with a distinctive edge.

Book Club Fiction

We are interested in accessible, discussion-worthy fiction that blends strong storytelling with layered themes.

Speculative Fiction

We enjoy genre-blending work with imaginative concepts, especially when grounded by human stakes and clear narrative focus.

Our approach

We work closely with writers to develop standout manuscripts and build lasting publishing careers. Our focus is on strong editorial guidance, careful positioning, and long-term author development.

Whether you are querying your first novel or preparing a new project, we value originality, clarity, and emotional resonance.
